## How do I get it?

Germinal is available [on github](https://github.com/Keruspe/Germinal)

Version 28 release tarball is available [here](https://www.imagination-land.org/files/germinal/germinal-28.tar.xz), get it while it's hot!

## What's new in this release?

The headline addition is **in-buffer text search**. Press `Ctrl+F` to open the search bar, type your pattern, and use `Ctrl+G` or `Enter` to jump to the next match, `Ctrl+Shift+G` for the previous one. `Escape` closes the bar and returns focus to the terminal. Search is case-insensitive and wraps around automatically.

This release also adds **trackpad zoom**: hold `Ctrl` and scroll to zoom in or out, the same as with a mouse wheel. A fix to keyboard shortcut controller precedence rounds things out.

Full keybindings are documented [here](https://github.com/Keruspe/Germinal/blob/master/README.md).

I hope you'll enjoy it. Feel free to propose new features and/or to contribute!
