It's available [there](http://www.imagination-land.org/files/gpaste/gpaste-3.12.tar.xz), get it while it's hot!

## What's new?

- Improved compatibility with GNOME 3.12

And don't forget to run `gpaste dr` aka `gpaste daemon-reexec` after upgrading GPaste to activate new functionalities ;)

You then can run `gpaste daemon-version` to check that the correct daemon is running.
