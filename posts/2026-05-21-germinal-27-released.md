## How do I get it?

Germinal is available [on github](https://github.com/Keruspe/Germinal)

Version 27 release tarball is available [here](https://www.imagination-land.org/files/germinal/germinal-27.tar.xz), get it while it's hot!

Keybindings are documented [here](https://github.com/Keruspe/Germinal/blob/master/README.md).

## What's new in this release?

- Ported to GTK4 and libadwaita
- Right-click menu ported to GMenuModel and GSimpleActionGroup
- Window title now uses VTE termprop API
- Scroll zoom gated to discrete wheel events only
- Minimum GLib 2.76, GTK 4.8, VTE 0.78, libadwaita 1.0

I hope you'll enjoy it. Feel free to propose new features and/or to contribute!
