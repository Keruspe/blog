GPaste 2.99.2 released

by Marc-Antoine Perennou on January 22, 2013

Tagged as: GPaste, release, clipboard, gnome.

Followup to last week’s release, here is a new bugfix release.

It’s available there, get it while it’s hot!

What’s new?

  • Fix a bug making gpaste settings ui a no-op
  • Don’t close legacy applet menu when deleting an item
  • Fix vala bindings
  • Updated portuguese translation

This is 3.0 RC3 and should be the latest RC before the 3.0 release.

Feedback really appreciated!

And don’t forget to run gpaste dr aka gpaste daemon-reexec after upgrading GPaste to activate new functionalities ;)
