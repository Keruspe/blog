## How do I get it?

Germinal is available [on github](https://github.com/Keruspe/Germinal)

Version 29 release tarball is available [here](https://www.imagination-land.org/files/germinal/germinal-29.tar.xz), get it while it's hot!

## What's new in this release?

The headline addition is a **built-in Preferences dialog**, accessible from the gear icon in the header bar or the right-click context menu. It covers all settings in one place: font, foreground and background colours, the full 16-colour palette, scrollback length, word character exceptions, audible bell, window decorations, startup command, and TERM variable. Every individual setting has a reset-to-default button.

The Preferences Appearance page includes a new **palette editor** showing all 16 ANSI colours as two rows of eight swatches (Normal and Bright), each directly editable.

This release also switches the **default colour theme to Dracula** — foreground `#f8f8f2`, background `#282a36`, and the full Dracula 16-colour ANSI palette — replacing the old arbitrary defaults.

Full keybindings are documented [here](https://github.com/Keruspe/Germinal/blob/master/README.md).

I hope you'll enjoy it. Feel free to propose new features and/or to contribute!
